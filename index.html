About Us

Our institution believes that efficient ways of transportation in turn results in better productivity.

Thus SGCET provides 16 buses for private transportation from all over the city to the campus. From over 16 different routes , the fleet carries students across trivandrum and nearby to the campus. All the buses are equipped with GPRS system for tracking and safety of the students. The timings and stops are detailed by the route.
